Hi there. I am Sayan Roy and I am currently a Chemical Engineering first year Student at Jadavpur University.

Though my branch is as not a IT branch as Computer Science and Engineering or Information Technology, but I am pursuing a career in Computer Science & Engineering, as Web Developer & Software Developer.

I am from Arambagh which is a small but beautiful city which is located in Hooghly district, West Bengal India. I love my city very much.

I passed my West Bengal Board of Secondary Education in 2017 from Arambagh High School and scored 90% and HS Examination also from Arambagh High School in 2019 and passed with 85.8%.

One of my family members is an Electronics & Telecommunication Engineer. He is now in Singapore. I also dreamed to be an Engineer. After first board examination, I took Science and I started preparing for engineering examinations . But at the end of class 11, many people wrote in blogs, newspaper that there are no-more job or opportunities in Engineering fields. So I changed my plan.

I missed my chance in Ramakrishna Mission Vidyamandira Belur Howrah for 0.5marks. But I had a fascination for Jadavpur University from the very beginning and I also got that opportunity. But at that very time, the WBJEE result came out and as I love Chemistry and also Engineering, I opt Chemical Engineering in Jadavpur.

I also cracked JEE Main 2019, KIITEE 2019, NCHM JEE 2019.

Beside my Chemical Engineering studies, I am also interested in how the Websites work, how computers work, how Applications work. So, I love the programming world in Computer and I took the course to gain skills in this field personally. My parents support me very much. I love them.
